üç≥ Greek Yogurt Bowl

Ingredients:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Honey
  • Blueberries
  • Granola
  • Chopped almonds

Preparation:

  1. Spoon Greek yogurt into a bowl.
  2. Drizzle honey evenly over the top.
  3. Add blueberries and sprinkle granola.
  4. Top with chopped almonds.
  5. Mix gently or keep layered. Serve chilled.

ü•™ Grilled Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken
  • Whole wheat tortilla
  • Avocado
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Hummus or mayo

Preparation:

  1. Grill the chicken and let it rest. Slice into thin strips.
  2. Warm the tortillas slightly in a pan or microwave.
  3. Spread hummus or mayo on the tortilla.
  4. Layer with lettuce, tomato, onion, avocado, and chicken.
  5. Roll the tortilla tightly and slice in half to serve.

üçé Apple with Peanut Butter
