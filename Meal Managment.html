📋 Meal Details

🍳 Greek Yogurt Bowl

Ingredients:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Honey
  • Blueberries
  • Granola
  • Chopped almonds

Preparation:

  1. Spoon Greek yogurt into a bowl.
  2. Drizzle honey evenly over the top.
  3. Add blueberries and sprinkle granola.
  4. Top with chopped almonds.
  5. Mix gently or keep layered. Serve chilled.

🥪 Grilled Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken
  • Whole wheat tortilla
  • Avocado
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Hummus or mayo

Preparation:

  1. Grill the chicken and let it rest. Slice into thin strips.
  2. Warm the tortillas slightly in a pan or microwave.
  3. Spread hummus or mayo on the tortilla.
  4. Layer with lettuce, tomato, onion, avocado, and chicken.
  5. Roll the tortilla tightly and slice in half to serve.

🍝 Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion, garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan & basil

Preparation:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, sauté chopped onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce, stir, and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Serve meat sauce over spaghetti and top with parmesan and basil.

🍎 Apple with Peanut Butter

Ingredients:

  • Apple slices
  • Peanut butter
  • (Optional) cinnamon or granola

Preparation:

  1. Wash and core the apple. Slice into thin wedges.
  2. Arrange slices on a plate.
  3. Serve with a dollop of peanut butter on the side or spread directly.
  4. Sprinkle with cinnamon or granola if desired.
