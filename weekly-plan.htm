Workout Plan

Monday: Cardio + Core

Running - 20 min
Moderate pace on treadmill or track. Great for warming up and torching fat.
Calories Burnt: ~200 kcal
Seated Crunches - 3x15
Focus on engaging the core while using the cable machine. Keep your elbows in place.
Calories Burnt: ~40 kcal
Leg Raises - 3x15
Focus on lifting your legs while keeping your back on the mat. Great for lower abs.
Calories Burnt: ~30 kcal
Russian Twist with Plates - 3x30s
Engage the core and obliques as you twist your torso, holding a plate in both hands.
Calories Burnt: ~40 kcal

Tuesday: Shoulders

Shoulder Press - 4x12
Focus on pressing the dumbbells or barbell overhead, keeping your core engaged.
Calories Burnt: ~70 kcal
Lateral Raises - 3x15
Lift the dumbbells to your sides, elbows slightly bent, targeting the deltoids.
Calories Burnt: ~50 kcal
Front Raises - 3x15
Raise the dumbbells or plate in front of you to shoulder height to engage the front delts.
Calories Burnt: ~50 kcal
Arnold Press - 3x12
A variation of the shoulder press, starting with palms facing you, then rotating outward.
Calories Burnt: ~70 kcal
Upright Row - 4x10
Pull the barbell or dumbbells straight up towards your chin, focusing on your traps and delts.
Calories Burnt: ~60 kcal
Rear Delt Fly - 3x12
With dumbbells in hand, hinge at your hips and raise your arms to the sides to target rear delts.
Calories Burnt: ~50 kcal
Shrugs - 4x12
Hold dumbbells or barbell at your sides and shrug your shoulders to engage your traps.
Calories Burnt: ~40 kcal

Wednesday: Kickboxing

Shadow Boxing - 3 rounds
Practice your punches and footwork without equipment to improve form and technique.
Calories Burnt: ~150 kcal
Heavy Bag - 5 rounds
Work on power and endurance with punches and kicks, aiming for maximum intensity.
Calories Burnt: ~250 kcal

Thursday: Full-body HIIT

Jump Squats - 3x15
Explosive lower-body exercise to increase power and burn fat.
Calories Burnt: ~100 kcal
Burpees - 4x10
High-intensity movement for full-body conditioning and cardiovascular endurance.
Calories Burnt: ~150 kcal
Push-ups - 3x20
Classic bodyweight exercise to build upper body strength.
Calories Burnt: ~50 kcal
Mountain Climbers - 4x30s
High-intensity core exercise that engages the entire body.
Calories Burnt: ~60 kcal

Friday: Strength Training

Deadlifts - 4x8
A compound lift targeting the posterior chain (lower back, glutes, hamstrings).
Calories Burnt: ~120 kcal
Bench Press - 4x10
Classic chest exercise for upper body strength development.
Calories Burnt: ~90 kcal
Barbell Rows - 4x10
Target the back muscles with controlled, rowing motions.
Calories Burnt: ~80 kcal
Overhead Press - 4x8
Build shoulder strength and stability with an overhead pressing motion.
Calories Burnt: ~75 kcal

Saturday: Active Recovery

Stretching - 15 min
Improve flexibility and reduce muscle tension after the week’s workouts.
Calories Burnt: ~40 kcal
Yoga Flow - 20 min
A calming, full-body stretch that aids in muscle recovery and mindfulness.
Calories Burnt: ~50 kcal
Walking - 30 min
Low-intensity activity to promote recovery and keep the body moving.
Calories Burnt: ~100 kcal

Sunday: Rest

Meditation
A calming practice to relax your mind and reduce stress for recovery.
Calories Burnt: ~15 kcal
